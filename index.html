Jimmy and Ingrid Baird of Passport People UK, formally Passport People cc, have been in the business of assisting with passports, visas and other related documents since October 1996.

Passport People UK now specialise in the application of the following BRITISH documentation.

  • British passports: First time applications and renewals.
  • British veteran passports: First time applications and renewals. For persons born before 2 Sept. 1929. No cost for passport.
  • British holiday and business visas: 6 months, 2 years, 5 years, and 10 year visas - no stay longer than 6 months.
  • British ancestral visas: For persons with a British born grandparent. Allows you to work for 5 years in the UK, after which you are able to apply for indefinite leave to remain, and then British citizenship by naturalisation.
  • British citizenship (UKM): Citizenship by registration, if your mother was born in the UK and you were born before 01-01-1983.
  • British citizenship (UKF): Citizenship by registration if you were born before 01-07-2006, your father was British born and he was NOT married to your mother at the time of your birth.
  • British birth/marriage/death certificates: For persons who were born, married or deceased in England, Wales, Scotland or Northern Ireland.
